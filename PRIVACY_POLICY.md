# Privacy Policy — Lumina Gallery

**Last updated: March 25, 2026**

## Overview

Lumina Gallery is a photo and video browsing app that connects to a server running on your personal computer over your local Wi-Fi network. Your privacy is our top priority.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.** Specifically:

- No analytics or tracking
- No user accounts or registration
- No data sent to any cloud service
- No advertising or ad tracking
- No cookies or web tracking
- No crash reporting sent to us

## How the App Works

- Lumina Gallery connects directly to a server running on your own computer over your home Wi-Fi network
- Photos and videos are transferred only between your iPhone and your computer
- No data passes through any third-party server
- All communication stays within your local network

## Camera Access

The app may request camera access solely to scan QR codes for connecting to a server. No photos or videos are captured or stored by the camera feature.

## Photo Library Access

The app may request access to your photo library solely to upload photos and videos to your personal server. Photos are sent directly to your computer — they are never uploaded to any external service.

## AI Features

The app uses Apple's on-device Vision framework to categorize photos. All AI processing happens entirely on your iPhone. No images or analysis results are sent anywhere.

## Data Storage

- Server connection details (IP address, access token) are stored locally on your device
- Upload history (which photos have been uploaded) is stored locally on your device
- No data is stored on any external server

## Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
